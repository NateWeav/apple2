macOS

macOS is the operating system that powers every Mac. It lets you do things you simply can’t do with other computers. That’s because it’s designed specifically for the hardware it runs on — and vice versa.

iCloud

iCloud safely and securely stores your photos, videos, documents, messages, music, apps and more — and keeps them updated across all your devices. So you always have access to what you want, wherever you want it.
