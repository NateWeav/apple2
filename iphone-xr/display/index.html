Beauty through innovation.

Our breakthrough LCD display is the most advanced — and most color-accurate — in the industry. Which makes everything you see look true to life.

The industry’s best color accuracy.

Advanced color management automatically displays your content in wide color. So everything you look at looks amazing.

True Tone.

A six-channel light sensor subtly adjusts the white balance onscreen to match the color temperature of the light around you. So images on the display look as natural as on a printed page and are easier on your eyes.

Resists water and dust.

The most durable front glass in a smartphone, sealed and precision-fitted with the aerospace‑grade aluminum band, helps create an enclosure that’s water resistant up to 1 meter for 30 minutes. iPhone XR even resists spills from coffee, tea, soda, and more.2
