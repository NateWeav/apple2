You can link to us using approved badges. Using them correctly is an important part of being a partner, so carefully follow the guidelines.

The best place to find links for Apple Music is the new Toolbox. The Toolbox provides embeddable widgets for albums, songs, and playlists as well as badge art. All links are mobile responsive and are optimized to be geo aware.
Go to the Toolbox

The best way to create links to apps, movies, TV shows, podcasts, and books is Link Maker. Link Maker also creates links to music, but the Apple Music Toolbox is optimized for creating Apple Music links.
Go to Link Maker
Go to the Apple Music Toolbox

Use the RSS Feed Generator to incorporate lists of the best-selling and most popular music, apps, books, and more.
Go to RSS Generator

By adding just one snippet of JavaScript code to your website, links will be automatically converted to an affiliate link.
Go to Auto Link Maker

Banner Builder lets you immediately create banner art for content on Apple Music as well as for movies, apps, and TV content on our stores.
Go to Banner Builder
