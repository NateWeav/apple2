Apple Leadership

Steve Dowling

Vice President,
Communications

Steve Dowling is Apple’s vice president of Communications, reporting to CEO Tim Cook.

Dowling is responsible for Apple’s worldwide media relations and communications strategy, leading the public relations team as well as employee communications and corporate events. He previously led Apple’s corporate public relations team for 10 years.

Before joining Apple in 2003, Dowling worked as a broadcast journalist at CNBC, first as a writer and producer in the network’s Washington, D.C. bureau.

He later established CNBC’s Silicon Valley bureau and served as bureau chief.

A native of Massachusetts, Dowling holds a bachelor’s degree in Political Science from the University of Minnesota.
