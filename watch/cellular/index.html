Apple Watch with cellular.
Stay connected when you’re away from your phone.

Apple Watch Series 4 and Series 3 with cellular allow you to make a call, send a text, and stream Apple Music from your wrist — all without your phone. To cover the LTE and UMTS bands used around the world, there are two separate models for Apple Watch Series 4, and three for Apple Watch Series 3.1 They have been optimized for use in the country of purchase, and not all models work in all countries or regions. There is no single model that supports roaming worldwide. To see which carriers in your country or region offer cellular service for Apple Watch, refer to the chart below.2

Jump to sectionApple Watch Series 4 Jump to sectionApple Watch Series 3

Check with your carrier to see if your iPhone plan is eligible for Apple Watch cellular service. If you have an enterprise or corporate cellular service plan, check with your company or carrier to see if they support this feature. Some pre–paid and some older accounts aren’t currently supported. To activate cellular service on new carrier networks, customers first need to update iPhone to the latest version of iOS 12 and Apple Watch Series 4 (GPS + Cellular) and Apple Watch Series 3 (GPS + Cellular) to the latest version of watchOS 5.
