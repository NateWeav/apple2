It’s part of the daily regimen for runners around the world, and now there are even more reasons to make the Nike Run Club app part of yours. Get a new Audio Guided Run every week with the voice of a guest coach and a curated soundtrack. View detailed in-run stats and metrics. And enjoy encouragement with new custom cheers.

With over 180 free workouts featuring world-class Nike Master Trainers, you can tap into a multitude of drills to help round out your fitness and augment your runs. In addition, notifications provide workout recommendations to close your Exercise ring for the day.
