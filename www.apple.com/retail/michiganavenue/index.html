Apple Michigan Avenue

A hub of creativity.
Right on the river.

Built along the Chicago riverfront, Apple Michigan Avenue is here to educate and inspire the city’s next generation of creative pioneers. It’s a space for the community to come together, connect, and share their talent and innovation.

Come together. Get inspired.

The Forum, centered around a 6K video wall, is a gathering place where you can learn, get inspired, and share your creativity.

The Avenue is where you’ll discover curated products and accessories for a wide range of creative interests, from photography, music, and gaming to art, design, and app development.

The Plaza, located in the center of historic Pioneer Court, sits above Chicago’s Riverwalk promenade and is open to the public 24 hours a day.

The Genius Grove is set under a canopy of trees and offers panoramic views of the Chicago River, so you can have all your questions answered in a relaxing environment.

Designed with every
angle in mind.

The wide, tiered staircase elegantly integrates Apple Michigan Avenue with the Plaza, connecting the store to the city outside and creating a place for the community to come together.

A 17 foot carbon fiber roof overhang provides shade while allowing for maximum light and transparency.

Apple Michigan Avenue is covered in transparent glass that reaches 32 feet high and is curved on all four corners, creating a seamless transition between the store and the city outside.

The interior features sandblasted limestone paired with white oak ceilings for a warm, welcoming environment.

How to get here:

Apple Michigan Avenue is located at 401 N. Michigan Ave, on the NE corner of the intersection of N. Michigan Ave and the Chicago River.Parking: in the 401 N. Michigan Ave building off of Lower Water Street.Public Transit: CTA stops: Grand Red Line, and State/Lake Brown, Green, Purple & Orange Lines are within blocks of the store. Water taxi access to Metra trains and Navy Pier are within a block of the store. A Divvy Bike Stop is available outside the store location.
