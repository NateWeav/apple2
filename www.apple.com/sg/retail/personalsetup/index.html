Personal Setup.
Only from Apple.

Get up and running online or in the store.

When you shop at an Apple Store or on apple.com, we’ll help you start enjoying your Apple product straightaway. Personal Setup is offered exclusively through Apple to answer your questions, walk you through key features, help you find great apps and show you how to personalise your device.

What’s covered with Personal Setup.

Apple Watch

Set up and personalise your new Apple Watch with calendars, notifications and more. Then add apps for health, fitness, news, travel or entertainment.

iPhone

Transfer your contacts; set up email accounts, calendars and Find My iPhone; do more with favourites from the App Store and handy camera tips.

Mac

Get a tour of useful features. Set up email and calendars, and learn how to install apps. Also, get more out of your Mac with the latest updates in macOS and iCloud.

iPad

Learn shortcuts, find apps and sync media from Photos, iTunes or Apple Music. Then set up email and social media accounts, and personalise your iPad.

Genius Bar and Support

Forgot your Apple ID or have another concern? Answer a few questions and we’ll quickly connect you with an expert via phone, chat or email.

Get software help

For hardware issues, like a cracked screen, make a Genius Bar reservation, visit an Apple Authorised Service Provider or mail your device to us. Just answer a few questions to get started.

If your Mac, iPad, iPhone or Apple Watch was purchased within the last 90 days from an Apple Retail Store, on apple.com or through 1800-692-7753 you are entitled to a Personal Setup session at the Apple Retail Store or online. Purchases from a reseller of Apple products are not eligible.

Sessions are subject to availability and are held on a first-come, first-served basis. Personal Setup may include the transfer of your contacts, setting up your email, showing you how to download apps and customising your product. Apple does not guarantee the transfer and/or setup of all data as certain data may not be supported. For instance, Personal Setup does not include the transfer of photos, music or other digital media. Sessions start promptly and end at their scheduled times. Arriving late to your session may result in its cancellation.

In addition, most Apple software and hardware products include unlimited complimentary phone support for incidents within the first 90 days of product ownership. See the support-related materials included with your Apple product for more information. Complimentary phone support provides support for basic setup, installation, assembly, connectivity, launch or reinstallation (but not data recovery) when your hardware configuration meets the minimum system requirements for the software. For further details on complimentary phone support, visit www.apple.com/support/complimentary.

Please note that when transferring data between devices there is a risk of data loss, corruption or breach. Therefore, if you request Personal Setup services, YOU ACCEPT AND AGREE THAT APPLE IS NOT RESPONSIBLE FOR ANY LOSS, CORRUPTION OR BREACH OF DATA STORED ON YOUR PRODUCT(S) AND IT IS YOUR RESPONSIBILITY TO MAKE A SAFE COPY OF YOUR DATA BEFORE BRINGING YOUR PRODUCT(S) TO APPLE FOR SERVICE.
