Apple TV App Everything you watch. All in one place.

The Apple TV App lets you browse content from over 100 video services without switching from one app to the next. You’ll find movies and shows, handpicked recommendations, and live sports and news. The Apple TV App is already on iPhone, iPad, and Apple TV — so you can watch at home or wherever you go.*

Watch Pose on FXNOW. Pose is available on iTunes

Watch wherever you go.

The Apple TV App works seamlessly across iPhone and iPad. So if you stop watching a movie or show halfway through on your Apple TV, you can pick it up later when you’re on the go. And now you can share your favorite shows and movies with friends directly from the app.

There’s always something good on the Apple TV App.

You’ll find your iTunes purchases, your subscription services, and a wide variety of free content on the Apple TV App. It even offers handpicked recommendations. Everything you want to watch is right here.

Watch Now serves up your favorite shows. It features Up Next, which lets you pick up where you left off, and What to Watch, where you’ll find handpicked recommendations.

The Sports tab is your ticket to live baseball, tennis, and more, including breaking scores and top events from winter sports to college basketball finals. Customize your experience to receive alerts for your favorite teams. Set your lineup of events to watch. And let Siri provide the assist — tune in to a game just by asking.

Everything you’ve purchased from iTunes, along with your current rentals, shows up in your Apple TV App library. And if an HD title in your library is released in 4K HDR, it is automatically upgraded for free.

The Store tab is the best place to find the latest video services and the content they offer. You can also find new movies, shows, and more on iTunes.

Find movies and shows by entering a title or actor in the Search tab. Or ask Siri anytime — no matter what you’re looking for.

The Apple TV App is already on your iPhone, iPad, and Apple TV.
